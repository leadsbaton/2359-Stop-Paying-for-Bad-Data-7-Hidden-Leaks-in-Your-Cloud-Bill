Stop Paying for Bad Data: 7 Hidden Leaks in Your Cloud Bill

As Snowflake footprints expand, organizations are under growing pressure to prove value, control spend, and deliver consistent performance. But FinOps dashboards often miss the real problem: not just compute, but waste. This session covers which design choices actually reduce compute cost goes and how to avoid runaway costs but it will also look beyond and uncover six other hidden ways your platform quietly burns cash, from processing bad data and duplicate tables to the hidden "re-run tax" on failed pipelines.

What We'll Cover:

  • The 7 hidden cost drivers your FinOps dashboard isn't showing you.
  • The Snowflake metrics that matter: scan volume, warehouse utilization, query efficiency, and storage/compute balance
  • Why bad data, ingestion sprawl, and shadow copies multiply compute.
  • How to stop paying the "re-run tax" by preventing pipeline failures.
  • Real examples: faster dashboards through re-clustering, 40% compute reduction through workload separation, and more

Why Attend: If your Snowflake bill is rising despite optimization efforts, this session provides a new playbook. Learn to identify and stop the real leaks with tuning patterns and turning waste into savings and building a more cost-aware data culture.
